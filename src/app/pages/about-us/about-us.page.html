Green TCNJ

App Version: 4.7.0

Green TCNJ is an app focused on displaying and storing useful information in regards to a greener campus. You can search which materials can be discarded or recycled on campus, register for upcoming environmental events, and view the latest recycling and environmental news for TCNJ.
