Green TCNJ

App Version: 1.1.2

Green TCNJ is a mobile application developed for the TCNJ Department of Environmental Health and Safety.

It is an app focused on displaying and storing useful information in regards to a greener campus. You can search which materials can be discarded or recycled on campus, register for upcoming environmental events, and view the latest recycling and environmental news for TCNJ.

The following people contributed to the design, development, maintenance, and enhancements of the Green TCNJ mobile application. Each participated in mentored research and served as a student research assistant in the Knox Laboratory.
